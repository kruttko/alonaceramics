About

Hi — I’m Alona Krutko. I make functional ceramics with a quiet, modern silhouette and tactile details (subtle carving, matte breaks, soft edges).

My studio work focuses on calm, neutral surfaces — warm beige, stone grey, and deep brown — inspired by wood, rock, and winter light.
